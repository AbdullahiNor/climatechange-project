Act Now

Climate Change is the defining issue of our time and we are at a defining moment. From shifting weather patterns that threaten food production, to rising sea levels that threatens our most populated areas and cities the impacts of climate change are global and will be felt by every one in an unprecedented in scale.

Yes We can

Yes we can, with drastic action and commitment today, adapting to these impacts in the future will be more easy and less costly. We can all contribute by reducing our footprint: Drive less, fly less, buy locally, reuse and recycle.

and if necessary protest in front of parliaments like Stortinget.
